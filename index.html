About Me

I am Andrea Vicari, I was born on March 10th 1995 in Enna, which is a small town in the middle of the Sicily, in the Southern part of Italy. I have been living there for 19 years.

After having attended the Liceo Classico in Enna, I decided to move to Lugano in order to join the faculty of informatics at USI. The Liceo Classico is school with an emphasy on humanities, so I am not used to scientific subjects but I am really motivated in reaching my goals and do all my best.

I have been interested in Informatics since 2004, when my parents first bought me a Personal Computer. I used to spent my days trying to figure out how this incredible machine worked and maybe, some day, be able to create programs all by myself. In my family I am regarded as the one who can do everything with a keyboard and a mouse. I think that they exagerate with their considerations, but I wish that other people, one day, would say the same about me.

Finally, after more or less 10 years, I had the possibility to discover this world deeper than before. I completed my studies for achieving the Bachelor degree in June 2017 and I am enrolled to the master program offered by USI on "Software & Data Engineering".

"Think big. Life’s too short to think small."
